The Hostel

Barak, the 7th hostel in the IITG fraternity, derives it's name from the River Barak. It is a major river in north-eastern India and is a part of the Surma-Meghna River System. It rises in the Manipur hills and enters the plains near Lakhipur. The two time General Championship Winner hostel brings a cheerful environment to all its boarders. In its very first year, the hostel created history by securing third place in inter hostel sports meet. Thus it showed its muscle power in sports and announced itself as the future hub of sports champions. Boarders of the hostel, as they call themselves, barakites are all set to show their spirit. "Come on guys. Its show time. Show the spirit, the everlasting spirit. Because Champs are here."

Hostel Mess

From the rugged terrain of the west to the occeanic adventure of the east and from the chillness of the north to the serene land of the south, the fooding experience of the barak mess takes you through all these food cultures. The mess offers a huge variety of food items from acoss all parts of the country. To ensure the satisfaction of students from all the different parts of the country, the hostelites elect a mess convener who in turn appoints a committee under him. The committee consists of students from all parts of india. They inspect the food quality and advice the convener accordingly.IITG has mess coupon and subscription systems available.

The Barak hostel also has a canteen and a juice centre to cater to the vast appetites of the hostelites.

Library

"Books are the doors to another universe". This was what a great man oonce said andwe Barakites totally adher to this philosophy. The barak library can be said to be a reading mind's paradise. The Air-Conditioned Library contains a wide range of books that can subject a mind to dimensions of thinking. Fantasy, fiction, biographical, autobiographical and what not. You name it and you will find it. So lets not confine ourselves when the mind can wander indefinitely without fear.

Music Room

The music room of Barak hostel has been carefully designed to breed novice budding talents in music. The music room consists of a keyboard, electric guitar, amplifier and drums.

Sports & Entertainment

Sports are an integral part of an IITian's life.So Barak hostel provides a sports room to the students for indoor games like Table Tennis, carrom and chess. The hostel has a Volleyball and a cricket ground. All sorts of sports equipments are available in the sports room.

The hostel also has a TV room with a 56-inch 4K LED TV and a home theater system for the boarders to watch their favourite sports and tournaments.
